Site Manager / Project Manager — Madrid

At Wolfblanc, we coordinate design, technical documentation and construction as one continuous process. We are looking for a site-management professional who sees architecture as an ally, not an obstacle.

RoleSite Manager / Project Manager
LocationMadrid, Spain
StudioWolfblanc Architects

Responsibilities

  • Coordination of construction and high-end finishes
  • Management of contractors, suppliers and certifications
  • Programme, budget and on-site quality control
  • Technical coordination with the architecture team
  • Client communication during the construction phase

Profile

  • Degree in Architecture, Building Engineering or a related field
  • 3–6 years of experience in residential or commercial construction
  • Experience in full-renovation projects
  • Leadership and on-site problem-solving ability
  • Working English proficiency
  • Knowledge of construction legislation and regulations in Madrid

What we offer

  • High-quality projects and demanding clients
  • Technical support team including architects and remote project management
  • Direct visibility of the results of your work
  • Competitive salary and conditions based on experience
How to apply
Send your CV and references from previous projects to info@wolfblanc.com with the subject line “Site Manager Madrid”.
