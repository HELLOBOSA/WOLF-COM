Residential Architect — Madrid

Wolfblanc Architects is an architecture studio registered in Spain (COAM 25160), Sweden and Greece. We deliver high-quality residential projects for private homeowners, investors and institutions.

RoleArchitect
LocationMadrid, Spain
StudioWolfblanc Architects

Responsibilities

  • Single-family and multi-residential projects
  • High-end full renovations
  • Technical coordination and documentation
  • Site supervision and quality control
  • Direct client communication and project management

Profile

  • Degree in Architecture — registered or in the registration process
  • 2–5 years of experience in architecture studios
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD, Revit and/or ArchiCAD
  • Advanced English (our working language is English)
  • Initiative, aesthetic judgement and technical rigour
  • Previous residential project experience is valued

What we offer

  • Real project responsibility with principal-level guidance from day one
  • A small team with genuine responsibility and visibility
  • Opportunity to grow within an internationally expanding studio
  • Direct contact with international clients
  • Competitive salary based on experience
