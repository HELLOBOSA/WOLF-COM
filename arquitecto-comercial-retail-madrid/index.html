Commercial & Retail Architect — Madrid

At Wolfblanc, we design commercial, restaurant and hospitality spaces as business tools. We are looking for someone with commercial sensitivity and experience in retail, F&B or workplace projects.

RoleCommercial Architect / Interior Designer
LocationMadrid, Spain
StudioWolfblanc Architects

Responsibilities

  • Design of retail, restaurant and hospitality spaces
  • Development of spatial concepts and brand briefs
  • Technical coordination: MEP, acoustics, lighting and extraction
  • Documentation and opening-permit management
  • Site management and coordination with specialist contractors

Profile

  • Degree in Architecture or Interior Design
  • 2–5 years of experience in commercial or hospitality projects
  • Commercial sensitivity and a client-focused approach
  • Proficiency in Revit, AutoCAD or ArchiCAD
  • Advanced English is essential
  • Portfolio of commercial or F&B projects

What we offer

  • Studio-led projects with direct clients, not subcontracted work
  • An international environment with projects in Spain, Sweden and Greece
  • Genuine responsibility from the start of each project
  • A small team and a high-quality working environment
  • Competitive salary based on experience and portfolio
How to apply
Send your CV and commercial-project portfolio to info@wolfblanc.com with the subject line “Commercial Architect Madrid”.
