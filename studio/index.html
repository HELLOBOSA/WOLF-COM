Architecture studio

Wolfblanc is an architecture and design studio in Spain, Sweden and Greece. We support homeowners, second-home buyers, investors and businesses with a full-service approach and a rigorous delivery method.

Wolfblanc Architects

Wolfblanc brings together Mediterranean design and an orderly way of working, with detailed technical documentation, BIM coordination and well-being criteria. We work across architecture, landscape, urban design, interiors, hospitality and real estate investment, from concept to completion or as expert support at a specific stage.

What Wolfblanc is

We are a registered architecture studio, with studios in Spain, Sweden and Greece. Our work ranges from residential projects to real estate investment, including hospitality and commercial spaces. Every project is backed by professional registration, proven experience and clear communication in several languages.

Who we work with

We work with four types of client, each with different needs and the same level of attention.

Homeowners

Housing, full renovation and interior design with a clear process and a single point of contact from start to finish.

Second homes

Villas and second homes designed around landscape, materials and durability, including for clients managing the project from abroad.

Investors and developers

Architecture that improves asset performance, with technical due diligence, floor plan efficiency and measurable return.

Business and hospitality

Restaurants, hotels, retail and offices where the space communicates identity, improves operations and increases value.

What we do

We offer nine services organised across three areas: spatial disciplines, experience design, and technical and strategic consultancy. They can be engaged as a whole or by stages.

Service Description
Architecture New buildings, rehabilitation and transformation, from early analysis through technical design, construction and handover. Total renovation and project management are integrated within this discipline.
Landscape architecture Places and living systems integrating use, planting, water, materials, climate and construction.
Urban design and masterplanning Sites, districts and public environments shaped by a clear spatial strategy.
Residential interiors Interior spaces tailored to daily use, with attention to light, material and atmosphere.
Commercial and hospitality interiors High-performance environments for brands and businesses: hospitality, retail, showrooms and offices.
Architectural visualisation Visual material for decisions, permits, sales and investor communication.
Technical due diligence Pre-purchase analysis to understand a property before committing capital.
BIM consultancy Digital coordination to reduce risk and turn project data into more precise decisions.
Well-being consultancy WELL AP criteria to measure and improve air, light, acoustics and comfort.

The people who design the project are the people who see it through, decision by decision.

How we work

  1. We listenWe understand the project, the needs, the budget and the opportunities of the site.
  2. We designWe develop and visualize the architectural concept and the decisions on material and atmosphere.
  3. We plan in detailWe prepare drawings, details, technical documentation and the permit strategy.
  4. We deliverWe accompany the construction with control of timelines and budget through to project handover.
