Stay informed throughout your project.

Wolfblanc offers a secure and simple way to keep clients informed about their projects. From concept to completion, our approach gives you clarity, transparency and peace of mind — even when you cannot be on site.

Wolfblanc Architects

How it works

After project setup and briefing, we run structured site visits at agreed intervals. Each visit produces a written report covering construction progress, quality observations, open technical queries, budget status and the next decision points requiring your input.

What is included

Photo documentation of each site visit. Written summary report within 48 hours. Budget tracking against the approved tender. Quality review of materials, finishes and workmanship. Technical query log — open, resolved and pending. Programme update against the agreed completion date.

Who it is for

Overseas and remote clients who own property in Spain, Sweden or Greece. International investors managing construction from another country. Buyers who have purchased off-plan and want independent oversight of delivery quality. Clients who have appointed their own contractor but want architect-level eyes on site.

Our commitment

We act in your interest, not the contractor's. Our reports are honest, structured and timed so that decisions can be made before problems escalate. All communication is in English and delivered within agreed response times.
