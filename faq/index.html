Frequently asked questions

Clear answers about working with Wolfblanc across Spain, Sweden and Greece: local authority, international clients, services, permits, investment and how to begin.

The studio

International studio, local authority

What is Wolfblanc?

Wolfblanc is an architecture and design studio working across Spain, Sweden and Greece. We design and coordinate residential, landscape, hospitality, commercial, cultural and development projects, from early strategy and concept design to permits, technical documentation and construction support.

Is Wolfblanc legally authorised to sign architecture projects in Spain, Sweden and Greece?

Yes. The studio holds verifiable professional registrations in Spain (COAM N.25160), Sweden (SAR/MSA) and Greece (TEE-TCG N.168011). Professional responsibility and sign-off are organised according to the local rules and scope of each project.

What makes Wolfblanc different from other architecture studios?

We combine international communication with local architectural authority. Our method joins design clarity, experience from major Nordic practices, BIM coordination, technical documentation, decision control and WELL AP wellbeing criteria. The value is not only language: it is turning a site, budget and local approval process into a controlled project path.

Where does Wolfblanc work?

We work across Spain, Sweden and Greece, with offices and teams in Madrid, Barcelona, Marbella, Stockholm, Athens and Thessaloniki. Our location pages also cover Mallorca, Valencia and Galicia. For international commissions beyond these markets, we can lead strategy, design, BIM and coordination alongside a local architect where required.

What languages does Wolfblanc work in?

We work routinely in English and Spanish and can also communicate in Swedish and Greek. This reduces language barriers and technical misunderstandings for owners, investors, operators and teams making decisions from another country.

Who does Wolfblanc work with?

We work with private homeowners, international buyers, second-home owners, developers, investors, family offices, construction companies, hospitality operators, commercial brands and institutions. Some arrive with a defined brief; others with a property or opportunity that needs architectural direction before decisions are made.

Homeowners

Housing and renovation

I am a homeowner and want to renovate. Is Wolfblanc right for me?

Yes. We support homeowners in full renovations, interior design and new-build projects, with a clear process from start to finish. We define the brief, design, prepare the technical documentation and supervise the works through to handover, with a single point of contact throughout.

Does Wolfblanc handle building permits?

Yes. We prepare the technical documentation and process the permit with the relevant local authority. In Madrid, Barcelona, Marbella and other municipalities, some interior renovations can be processed through prior notification or a responsible declaration, which shortens timelines significantly.

What is the process of a project with Wolfblanc?

The process has four phases. First we listen, to understand the project, needs, budget and possibilities. Then we design and visualize the concept. Next we plan the drawings and construction decisions in detail. Finally we deliver, accompanying the project through to completion.

Second homes

Villas and international clients

I want to build or renovate a villa on the Costa del Sol. How do you work?

We design villas and second homes in Marbella, Benahavís, Estepona and across the Costa del Sol, with attention to landscape, orientation, materials and durability. We coordinate the project from design through construction supervision, and can start from a technical due diligence if the property already exists.

I am managing the project from abroad. Is that a problem?

No. Many of our clients manage their project in Spain remotely. We work with clear communication, visualizations, regular reports and well-ordered documentation so decisions can be made without needing to be on site. We work in several languages to make follow-up easier.

Investors and developers

Investment and real estate development

Does Wolfblanc work with investors and developers?

Yes. We are a strong fit for investors, developers and family offices who want architecture to improve asset performance. We think about floor plan efficiency, use mix, natural light, material durability and appeal for sale or rental, with measurable return.

What is pre-purchase technical due diligence?

It is a technical analysis that helps buyers and investors understand a property before committing capital. We review structure, services, damp, condition and hidden planning potential, and set out the findings and their estimated cost in a clear report so decisions can be made with judgement.

Commercial and hospitality

Business, brands and hospitality

Does Wolfblanc design shops, restaurants and hotels?

Yes. We work in hospitality, retail, offices and mixed-use spaces. For commercial interiors and brand spaces we collaborate with EOLOS Design Lab, our specialized division. The aim is for the space to communicate identity, improve operations and increase business value.

Do you handle activity licences for hospitality?

Yes. For restaurants, boutique hotels and commercial premises we prepare the activity project and coordinate the licence process with the town hall, alongside the design and construction supervision. We offer concept, permits and delivery as one integrated service.

Method and fees

How we work

What is WELL AP certification and why does it matter?

WELL AP is an accreditation in design for human health and well-being. It lets us consider air quality, natural light, acoustics, thermal comfort and materials from the start of the project. In housing it improves daily comfort, and in hotels, offices and clinics it supports experience and operational value.

Does Wolfblanc work with BIM methodology?

Yes. We use BIM workflows to coordinate architecture, services and interiors with digital precision. This reduces on-site conflicts, improves control of quantities and budget, and supports better-informed decisions at every phase of the project.

How are Wolfblanc's fees structured?

We work with a transparent fee model: a fee agreed at the outset, based on the scope of the project, with no hidden costs. In the first conversation we define the scope, the proposed services and the fee structure before starting.

How do I start a project with Wolfblanc?

The first step is a conversation about the project, the site, timelines, budget and goals. From there we assess the fit, the scope and the responsibilities, and propose the next steps. You can write to us at info@wolfblanc.com or use the contact form.
